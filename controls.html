Game Controls

Everything you need to rip and tear. Click the game canvas to lock your pointer, then go wild.

Movement

W Move forward
S Move backward
A Strafe left
D Strafe right
Shift Run (faster movement)
Ctrl Crouch (slower movement)
Space Jump

Look

Mouse Look around (requires pointer lock)
← → Turn left / right (keyboard)
↑ ↓ Move forward / backward (alt)

Combat

Left Click Fire weapon
X Fire weapon (alternate)
R Reload
1 Pistol
2 Shotgun
3 Rifle
Q Quick switch (Weapon 1)

Interaction & System

E Use / Interact
Click Canvas Lock pointer (start playing)
ESC Release pointer lock
F Toggle fullscreen mode
F1 Toggle debug overlay
